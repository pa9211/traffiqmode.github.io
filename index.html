About us

TraffIQ is a digital education platform designed to help students understand and recognize various traffic signs through an interactive, fun, and accessible approach. Through a combination of learning materials and interactive quizzes, TraffIQ offers an innovative solution for instilling traffic safety awareness from an early age. This website is designed not only to provide information but also to train users' critical thinking skills and traffic intelligence.
